COVID-19 (Coronavirus).

Michael Lindsey

March 6, 2020

Gulfport High School is aware that there are concerns about the outbreak of the respiratory illness caused by COVID-19(Coronavirus). Although there are currently no cases in Mississippi,we receive regular guidance from the Mississippi Department of Education who is in contact with other state agencies regarding preventive measures and preparations. This guidance includes basic personal protection and hygiene.

Personal Protection and Hygiene

 Hand Hygiene – Students and staff should wash hands often with soap and water. At GHS, soap is available in all bathrooms and there are hand sanitizers available at the point-of-sale registers in the cafeteria.  Respiratory etiquette – CDC recommends covering the nose and mouth with tissue when coughing or sneezing (or a shirt sleeve/elbow if not tissue is available) and throwing the tissue in the trash after use, followed by hand washing.  Routine Cleaning – Our custodians do an amazing job each day to keep our campus clean. In the past few weeks, our custodial staff has increased our routine cleaning efforts to ensure we routinely (multiple times a day) clean and disinfect frequently touched areas such as doors and the tops of desks and tables. Avoid touching your eyes, nose, and mouth with unwashed hands

Travel

In the next few months we have several groups who will be traveling out of state for academic and extracurricular functions. At this time there are no out of state travel restrictions for our students and staff. We will continue to monitor this situation closely and keep our coaches and sponsors informed if there are any changes.

The safety, health, and well-being of the GHS Staff and our students remain our highest priority. We will continue to monitor the developing situation and provide updates to our GHS Community as needed.
